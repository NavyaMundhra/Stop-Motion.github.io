Stop Motion Studio is an app that makes stop motion easy for beginners. It introduces the concept in a simple manner. It allows beginners to create their own little films and also provides tutorials for the same. It has many creative yet helpful features to help enhance each creators unique ideas. These features also help simplify the process. Some of them include audio, titles and credits, backgrounds, visual effects, speech and also facial expressions!

Stop Motion Studio provides creators with a range of sound effects and theme music. Various sounds such as "BANG" can be used in multiple scenes to keep the audience hooked and to make things look more realistic. Theme music can help communicate the genre of the movie across to the audience. What's more, it helps develop an idea of interest regarding the film.

The app also provides titles, credit pages and backgrounds. These help each project to seem more film-like. They also help bring out the genre of the film (like the audio). They have provided various styles and designs for the creators to chose from. It can go from simple to detailed, elegant to casual and it also allows the creators to customise and create their own designs.
